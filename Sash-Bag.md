#Sash Bags

Sash Bags are cross body bags designed to combine the convenience of a crossbody bag with the organization of a wallet. Worn comfortably across the body, they keep everyday essentials secure and easily accessible while leaving your hands free. Multiple compartments help organize cards, cash, phones, keys, and other necessities, making them ideal for travel, errands, walks, festivals, and everyday use. Available in a variety of colours, materials, and hardware finishes, Sash Bags are designed to be both practical and stylish.
